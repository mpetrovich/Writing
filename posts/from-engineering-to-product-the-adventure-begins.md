About two months ago, I became a product manager for the first time. Before, I had been in engineering for over a decade as a software engineer and engineering manager. The transition from engineering to product isn’t that common, and I didn’t find many others who talked about their experiences with similar career changes. I want to change that and share my story.

![](images/from-engineering-to-product-the-adventure-begins/01.jpeg)

Photo by [Darran Shen](https://unsplash.com/@darranshen)

Over the coming weeks and months, I’ll bring you along my journey into the land of product. It’ll be a magical, winding path full of wonders, traps, and surprises—and lots of learning. Grab some comfortable shoes, a warm drink, and hit that subscribe button. [Let’s go exploring!](https://www.gocomics.com/calvinandhobbes/1995/12/31)
